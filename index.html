• Pull: Pullups / assisted pullups on bar.
    • Row: Body rows under parallel or sloping bars.
    • Hang core: Hanging knee/leg raises or just dead hang.
    • Push: Pushups on ground or angled bar.
    • Dips: Parallel bar dips or assisted dips with feet.
    • Step-ups: Step onto pad, stand tall, step down.
    • Split squats: One leg forward, one back, bend both knees.
    • Plank: Forearms or hands on ground, straight body.
    • Hollow hold: On back, ribs down, legs/shoulders slightly up.
    • Calf raises: Lift heels up and down on a pad/edge.

    harder

    • Add backpack weight when things feel “easy”.
    • Use lower bars / more horizontal body for rows.
    • Feet-elevated pushups; deeper dips (pain-free range).
    • Slower lowers (3-4s down) on most reps.
    • More rounds or 1-2 extra reps (but still clean form).
